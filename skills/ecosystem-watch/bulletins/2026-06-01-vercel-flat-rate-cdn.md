## What Changed
Vercel launched Flat Rate CDN in Limited Beta for Pro teams. It replaces usage-based CDN pricing with a fixed monthly fee, offering more predictable infrastructure costs for high-traffic deployments. Full details at https://vercel.com/changelog/flat-rate-cdn-in-limited-beta.

## Impact on Agents
Check https://vercel.com/changelog/flat-rate-cdn-in-limited-beta for updated pricing patterns before recommending CDN cost estimates for Vercel-hosted projects. Pro teams with significant CDN usage should evaluate whether flat-rate is more cost-effective than current usage-based pricing.

## What Changed
OpenAI and Dell Technologies partnered (June 8, 2026) to make OpenAI models and Codex available in hybrid and on-premises enterprise environments — enabling air-gapped or private-cloud deployments for organizations that cannot use public cloud AI endpoints. Full details at https://openai.com/news/company-announcements/

## Impact on Agents
CKS agents building for enterprise clients with data-residency or compliance constraints should note this as a new viable deployment option for OpenAI-backed features. Check https://openai.com/news/company-announcements/ for deployment requirements and supported configurations before recommending cloud-only OpenAI integrations to regulated clients.
